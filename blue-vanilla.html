About

Blue Vanilla + Co. is a Personal Wellness Agency.

The first of its kind in Canada, we work with individuals and families in conjunction with Wellness Practitioners in non-pharmaceutical based treatments to carefully Assessments and align your Personal Wellness goals to right advisors, educational resources, therapeutic experiences and health services to help you achieve your goals.

Perhapps you want to supplement your drug treatment with healthy lifestyle changes, or maybe you want to complement your existing therapy regimen, maybe you want to put health prevention measures in place, maybe you are aware of the benefits of living a balanced lifsetyle...

Whatever your situation, we work as your personal health, Think of us as your partner in Personal Wellness.

We exist to help you make sense of all your options, providing you with well-vetted, professional practitioners and overseeing the coordination and implementation of your wellness strategies.

Our goal is to not only be your trusted advisor, but your friend and companion when it comes to you and your family living a healthier, more conscious lifestyle.

Connecting you with what you need, when you need it.
